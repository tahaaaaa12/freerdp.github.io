FreeRDP: A Remote Desktop Protocol Implementation

FreeRDP is a free implementation of the Remote Desktop Protocol (RDP), released under the Apache license. Enjoy the freedom of using your software wherever you want, the way you want it, in a world where interoperability can finally liberate your computing experience.

Privacy policy for aFreeRDP

What information do we collect?

We do only collect information from you when you agree to send a crash report in aFreeRDP.
A crash report may include:

  • Data of your remote session (IP, URL, Password, Username)
  • User statistics/Installation statistics over the Google Play store

How do we use the information?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used to improve aFreeRDP or to find and fix bugs.

What information do we share?

We do not sell, trade or transfer any collected information!
We may however share the crash report with developers. Crash-reports will be deleted immediately after analysis.

RDP Security

RDP Security depends on server configuration - Ask your network Administrator for further details.

Permissions

All permissions requested are required for proper operation of RDP remoting.

INTERNET

This is required to access public RDP servers

ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Required to let the application detect network connection availability or loss

ACCESS_WIFI_STATE

Required to let the application detect network connection availability or loss

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

Optional, required to allow opening RDP files and for sharing the SD-Card with a remote computer (RDP drive redirection)

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

Optional, required for sharing the SD-Card with a remote computer (RDP drive redirection)

MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS

Optional, used for RDP sound redirection

RECORD_AUDIO

Optional, required for microphone sharing (RDP microphone redirection)
